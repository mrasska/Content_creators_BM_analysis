# Video makers business models study
This repository gathers all the scripts used to collect contact information of YouTube French video makers and to analyse the data from the survey.

## Goal of this study
This paper presents the results of the business models of French video makers who published on YouTube and/or on Twitch. It also explores the factors of creators monetisation strategies success. The success was measured by the share of the creative income in the total 2021 income. 

## Presentation of the files
**Data_check.ipynb** is the script to check the consistency of participants answers to the survey. </p>
**Stat_desc.ipynb** gathers all the statics for the surveyed population. </p>
**MCA_coding_V_2023.ipynb** is the script cleaning and transforming the data for the MCA and clustering analysis. The output of this notebook gethers only on the participants who produces mainly for YouTube and/or Twitch. </p>
**MCA_cluestering.R** computes on R the MCA and HPCP analysis. It also adds the cluster column to the csv files. </p>
**Econometrics_coding.ipynb** gathers all the variables coding for the ordered logistic regression. *(The econometric analysis is performed on Stata MP.)*
